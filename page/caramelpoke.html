Matcha and Strawberry Slice

Featuring a matcha shortbread base topped with strawberry cheesecake, these slices not only look great, but they taste amazing.

Ingredients

  • Yellow cake mix
  • Heavy cream
  • Caramel sundae topping
  • Whipped topping
  • Salted caramel sauce, use homemade or store bought

Instructions

  1. Prepare the yellow cake mix as directed and bake.
  2. While the cake bakes combine the heavy cream, caramel sundae topping, and whisk to combine.
  3. Remove cake from the oven when its done and use a fork to poke holes all over the cake. Pour the caramel and cream mixture over the cake.
  4. Chill for about 15 minutes before spreading the whipped topping over the cake. Then, drizzle with salted caramel sauce.
  5. Cover cake and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before slicing and serving.
