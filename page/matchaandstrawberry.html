Matcha and Strawberry Slice

Featuring a matcha shortbread base topped with strawberry cheesecake, these slices not only look great, but they taste amazing.

Ingredients for Matcha Shortbread Base

  • 200g butter
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 180g plain flour
  • 250g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1tbsp matcha powder

Ingredients for Strawberry Cheesecake Filling

  • 450g cream cheese
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 1tbp strawberry jam
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 1- 2 drops red food colouring, optional
  • 250ml whipped cream

To serve

  • 8 strawberries, halved
  • Matcha powder to sprinkle

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 165C.
  2. Line base and sides of a 22 centimetre square baking tin with baking paper.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Mix through the vanilla followed by the flour and matcha powder, to form a dough.
  4. Press the dough into the base of the baking tin and bake for 20 minutes or until lightly golden. Set aside to cool.
  5. Prepare the cheesecake filling by whipping the cream cheese and sugar on high speed until smooth and creamy. Add the jam, vanilla, and food colouring, if using, to create your desired shade of pink. Gently fold through the whipped cream.
  6. Pour cheesecake filling over base and spread evenly. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  7. To serve, remove from the baking tin and cut into evenly sized pieces. Top each piece with a strawberry half and sprinkle lightly with matcha powder.
